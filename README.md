# Mario_Game
HOW TO PLAY - Super Mario Clone

1. HOW TO START THE GAME:
   - Double-click on "launcher.html" to open the game in your web browser
   - OR open your web browser and drag/drop "index.html" into it
   - Click the "Play Game" button

2. CONTROLS:
   - Left Arrow Key: Move Mario to the left
   - Right Arrow Key: Move Mario to the right
   - Up Arrow Key: Make Mario jump

3. GAME OBJECTIVE:
   - Collect all coins for points
   - Jump on enemies to defeat them
   - Avoid falling off the platforms
   - Reach the flag at the right side of the screen to complete the level

4. GAME ELEMENTS:
   - Red character: That's Mario (you!)
   - Gold coins: Collect these for 100 points each
   - Brown enemies: Jump on them to defeat them (200 points)
   - Brown brick platforms: Jump on these to navigate
   - Green pipes: Obstacles to avoid
   - Flag at the end: Reach this to complete the level

5. SCORING:
   - Each coin: 100 points
   - Each defeated enemy: 200 points
   - Completing the level: 500 points bonus

6. LIVES:
   - You start with 3 lives
   - You lose a life if you fall off the screen or get hit by an enemy
   - When all lives are lost, the game ends

TIPS:
- Time your jumps carefully to collect coins and defeat enemies
- Try to collect all coins for maximum points
- Jump on enemies from above to defeat them
- Don't jump into enemies from the side or you'll lose a life

Enjoy the game!
